About

Bringing the world to your kitchen!

Our food blog is an exploration of food in countries all around the world. We hope to showcase some of the incredible, delicious and wonderful things they eat in different countries around the world and we hope it inspires you to follow us throughout the journey

We have many recipes from every continent for you to try, many easy enough for a weeknight, others elegant enough for the weekend. By introducing foods from soo many countries into your meals (as well as a full calendar of holidays around the world), you will reclaim the joy and adventure of cooking with your family.

    Are you ready to explore the world through food?
    Do you dream of traveling across this glorious planet, but limited time or money makes that hard right now?
    Are you tired of eating the same boring meals? (I’m looking at you, steamed carrots, boiled corn, and baked potatoes!)
    Are you curious about trying foods from other countries, but don’t know where to start?

Then you are in the right place! Our blog is perfect for you, even for any beginners!
